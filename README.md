## â˜ï¸ About The Project
Welcome to a flight simulator where you have zero control over your speed. The plane moves forward automatically; your only job is to not crash.

You are piloting a tiny biplane through a canyon of massive, unforgiving monoliths. The gaps are tight, the speed is constant, and the only way out is through.

**The Mechanics:**
* **Constant Velocity:** You cannot slow down to aim. You have to react instantly.
* **Vertical Precision:** The gameplay is purely about altitude control. Thread the needle or hit the concrete.
